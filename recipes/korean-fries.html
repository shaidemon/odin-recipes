Korean chilli-cheese fries

Load French fries with Korean chilli flakes, gochujang, coriander and plenty of cheese for an indulgent sharing dish to serve at your next gathering

Ingredients

  • frozen oven french fries 600g
  • parmesan (or veggie alternative) 50g, finely grated
  • mature cheddar 50g, finely grated
  • garlic salt 1 tsp
  • mayonnaise 125g
  • gochujang 85g
  • rice vinegar 2 tsp
  • spring onions 2 large, finely sliced on the diagonal
  • coriander Â½ a small bunch, chopped
  • toasted sesame seeds 2 tbsp
  • Korean red pepper flakes 1 tbsp (optional)

Method

  1. Heat the oven to 220C/fan 200C/gas 7. Spread out the fries on a large baking tray. Bake for 15 minutes, then toss the fries with a fish slice or spatula, sprinkle over the grated cheeses and garlic salt, and bake for another 10-12 minutes or until golden and crisp.

  2. Meanwhile, mix together the mayonnaise, gochujang and vinegar.

  3. Use a spoon to drizzle the gochujang mayo all over the hot fries, alternating with sprinkles of spring onions, coriander, sesame seeds and pepper flakes, if using. Finish with a final drizzle of the spicy mayo.
